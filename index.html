How to play Lucky Dungeon

You are playing the hero who just entered Lucky Dungeon! You must defeat all the enemies in your way. After every enemy defeated you will get to select ONE item from the THREE displayed. Each item is different and has different value of stats. The new item will replace your current item equipped, weapon, armor, or ability item. You must pick one of the items show, even if all of your items currently equipped are better then the rewarded items displayed. Lets see how Lucky you are when rolling these items. How many enemies can you defeat?

You have three abilities. Basic Attack which has a base stat of 10 attack power. This has a 1 second cooldown. Then there is Ability Attack which has a base stat of 15 ability power. This has a 3 second cooldown. Lastly you have a Heal Ability. This will have a base stat of 7 and will heal your current HP. This has a 5 second cooldown.

Once you press the start button the first enemy will start attacking. So be ready and good luck! You're going to need it.

Congragulations on defeating the evil monster! Please select 1 of the 3 items below as a reward.
